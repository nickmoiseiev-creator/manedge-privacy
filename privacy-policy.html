Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

ManEdge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ManEdge (the "App").

Information We Collect

ManEdge is designed with privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Profile Information: Your name (optional) for personalization
  • Progress Data: Your learning progress, completed topics, and quiz results
  • Career Path Data: Your daily completion status, streak information, and freeze days used
  • Achievements: Unlocked achievements and experience points
  • Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, and notification preferences

How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress through the content
  • Provide gamification features (achievements, levels, streaks)
  • Save your preferences and settings

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your personal information on any external servers. Your data remains entirely under your control.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no external data transmission.

In-App Purchases

ManEdge offers subscription-based access through Apple's In-App Purchase system:

  • 3-day free trial
  • Monthly subscription: $4.99/month
  • Annual subscription: $49.99/year

All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

Push Notifications

With your permission, ManEdge may send push notifications for:

  • Career Path daily reminders
  • Quiz availability notifications
  • Motivational tips and learning reminders

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export all your data at any time through Settings → Data → Export Data
  • Delete: You can delete all your data through Settings → Data → Reset Progress
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

ManEdge is designed for professional restaurant managers and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (through the app interface)
  • Export your data (Settings → Export Data)
  • Delete your data (Settings → Reset Progress)
  • Opt-out of notifications (Settings → Notifications)

Third-Party Services

ManEdge uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple In-App Purchases: For subscription management (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): For sending notifications (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is primarily managed through your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device passcode/biometric authentication
  • Regularly backing up your data using the Export feature
